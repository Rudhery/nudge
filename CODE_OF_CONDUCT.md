# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

Nudge is a welcoming project. Everyone who participates — by reporting issues,
suggesting ideas, or contributing code — is expected to help keep it a
respectful and harassment-free space for all.

## Our standards

Examples of behavior that helps the community:

- Being kind, patient, and respectful.
- Giving and gracefully accepting constructive feedback.
- Focusing on what is best for the project and its users.
- Critiquing **code and ideas, not people**.

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Personal attacks, insults, or derogatory comments.
- Harassment of any kind, public or private.
- Publishing others' private information without consent.
- Any conduct that would reasonably be considered inappropriate.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies in all project spaces — issues, pull requests,
discussions — and whenever someone is representing the project in public.

## Enforcement

Concerns can be reported privately to the maintainer
[@Rudhery](https://github.com/Rudhery) on GitHub. Reports will be reviewed and
handled confidentially. Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove contributions, and
may temporarily or permanently ban anyone for behavior they consider
inappropriate.
